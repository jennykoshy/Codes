Welcome to the BMI Calculator!


This calculator provides BMI and the corresponding BMI weight status category. Use this calculator for adults, 20 years old and older. Body mass index (BMI) is a measure of body fat based on height and weight that applies to adult men and women. The body mass index or Quetelet index is a value derived from the mass and height of a person. The BMI is defined as the body mass divided by the square of the body height, and is universally expressed in units of kg/mÂ², resulting from mass in kilograms and height in metres.BMI can be used to indicate if you are overweight, obese, underweight or normal. A healthy BMI score is between 20 and 25. A score below 20 indicates that you may be underweight; a value above 25 indicates that you may be overweight.Enter your weight and height using standard or metric measures to calculate your correct Body Mass Index.
